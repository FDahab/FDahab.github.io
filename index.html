About Me

Hello! My name's Francisco Neme Campos Dahab, I am Master's student at the Institute of Theoretical Physics of the São Paulo State University (IFT-UNESP). My research currently focuses on Particle Physics, particularly I'm looking for ways new physics can manifest itself in future Lepton colliders.

This page serves two purposes. First, if you are interested in getting in touch or knowing what I work with, you can access the Contact tab, or you can find my curriculum below. But perhaps more interestingly, I'll try to compile a few nice problems and their solutions, as well as sometimes discuss a few nice ideas in topics I find fun. If you have any comments on any topics I discuss, or find any problems with my solutions, please do get in touch.
