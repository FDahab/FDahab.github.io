Schwinger-Dyson Equations, Time-Ordering, and the clash between the Path Integral and Canonical Formalisms

The true dictionary between objects in the Path Integral formalism and the Canonical Formalism is often not given a lot of emphasis on Quantum Field Theory textbooks. At some point, this imprecision gave me quite a bit of headache, particularly in the context of Schwinger-Dyson Equations, the idea of this discussion is to help me fully flesh out a few of these ideas in my head, and hopefully yield an interesting discussion as a byproduct.
